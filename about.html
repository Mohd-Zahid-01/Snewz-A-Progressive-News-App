About

Hello, welcome to the SNewz app means Simple News. It is a progressive web app that can also be used in offline mode, apart from this we can also install it in our system. It is a very lightweight app as compare to other apps. In This App, you can find the latest news, Top headlines from many sources. You can select sources from all over the world and search for any news with sorting by published, relevancy and date. It is powered by NewsAPI.This product is made by CanWeBe.If you want to join our group or see what we do please visit CanWeBe website.
